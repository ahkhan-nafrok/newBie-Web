About Wango — The Mango Club

Celebrating the king of fruits with passion, creativity, and authentic flavors

Wango started with one idea: celebrate the mango. Sourcing the ripest Alphonso and pairing it with modern desserts, we make mango treats that feel nostalgic and new at the same time. Our menu rotates seasonally to bring you the best textures and flavours.

Fresh Ingredients

Local farms • Handpicked daily • No artificial preservatives. We source directly from trusted farms to ensure every mango meets our quality standards.

Crafted Desserts

Shakes, puddings, cheesecakes & seasonal specials. Each creation is meticulously crafted to highlight the natural sweetness and texture of premium mangoes.

Open Kitchen Experience

Get a seat at our counter and watch the mango magic happen. Great for dates and small gatherings.
