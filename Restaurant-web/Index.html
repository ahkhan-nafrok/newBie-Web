Our Story

The Bella Vista Experience

Discover the passion and tradition behind our authentic Italian cuisine

A Culinary Legacy

Founded in 1998 by Chef Marco Bianchi, Bella Vista began as a small family restaurant in the heart of Milan. Chef Marco brought his passion for authentic Italian cuisine to New York, creating a space where tradition meets innovation.

Today, under the direction of Marco's daughter Sofia, we continue to honor our roots while embracing modern culinary techniques. Our kitchen is a place where time-honored recipes are preserved and new creations are born.

Our Philosophy

At Bella Vista, we believe that exceptional food begins with exceptional ingredients. That's why we source the finest produce from local farmers and import specialty items directly from Italy.

Our menu changes seasonally to showcase the freshest ingredients available. From handmade pasta to wood-fired pizzas, every dish is prepared with meticulous attention to detail and a deep respect for Italian culinary traditions.
