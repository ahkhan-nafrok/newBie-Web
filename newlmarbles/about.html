Who We Are

About Us

70+ Years of
Marble Mastery

For over seven decades, MSP Stone International has been at the forefront of premium marble craftsmanship in India.

Established in Makrana, Rajasthan - the birthplace of the world's finest marble - our legacy spans three generations of master artisans dedicated to transforming natural stone into timeless masterpieces.

From the iconic marble temples that grace homes across the globe to bespoke architectural installations, we bring together traditional craftsmanship and modern precision. Our expertise in custom mandirs, intricate jaali work, and premium flooring has earned us the trust of discerning clients worldwide.

With an extensive network across India and international presence in Dubai, USA, UK, Australia, and Tanzania, we continue to set the benchmark for excellence in the marble industry. Every piece that leaves our workshop carries the weight of our heritage and the promise of unparalleled quality.
