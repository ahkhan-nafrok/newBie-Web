OUR MINES

Our Makrana Sangemarmar marble mines are located in the historic town of Makrana, Rajasthan, globally renowned for producing the purest and finest quality white marble. This iconic marble has been used in world-famous monuments, including the Taj Mahal. Known for its exceptional strength, natural shine, and timeless elegance, Makrana Sangemarmar marble is carefully extracted and processed to maintain its unmatched purity and durability, making it a preferred choice for luxury architecture and interior spaces.
