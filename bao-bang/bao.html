About Us

Born in Bengaluru, inspired by Himalayan kitchens and bustling pan-Asian streets. Our story is one of tradition meeting innovation.

We believe in keeping it simple yet extraordinary: soft, cloud-like baos filled with authentic flavors, hand-pleated momos that carry centuries of tradition, and comforting bowls that warm both body and soul.

Our kitchen blends traditional Himalayan recipes with contemporary techniques, using seasonal ingredients and house-made sauces. Every dish is crafted with care, designed for both casual dining and memorable evenings.

— The BAO Family
